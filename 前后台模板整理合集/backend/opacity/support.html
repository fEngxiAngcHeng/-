F.A.Q.

How do I login?

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Type in your username and password
  3. If you forgot password, click forgot password

How do I copy/paste?

  1. Select a certain block of text
  2. Press CTRL + C to copy
  3. Choose where you want the copied text
  4. Press CTRL + V to paste

How do I submit a bug?

  1. Go to the support page
  2. Click the contact us button
  3. Make sure you put bug in the subject
