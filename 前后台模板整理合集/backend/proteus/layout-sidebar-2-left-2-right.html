2 Left and 2 Right Sidebars

  • Home
  • Layouts
  • Sidebar
  • Pixel Based
  • 2 Left and 2 Right

As always in life you can overcomplicate things as we did with this example.

Here we feature 2 left sidebars and 2 right sidebars, each of them serving a different purpose.

You can check out the functionality of the first left sidebar here.

The role of the second left sidebar is to serve us with important information which don't have to be visible all the time, in our case it's a secondary menu.

The first right sidebar shows a list of active users and the second right sidebar gives us information about our social status.

Notice how easy it was for us to set up 4 sidebars with different functionality and purpose with only 4 Ason Widget calls, each of them behave differently on different screen sizes.

For more information please check out the documentation!
